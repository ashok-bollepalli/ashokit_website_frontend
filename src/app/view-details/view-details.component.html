Frequently asked questions

How can I practice?

Students can benefit from our state-of-the-art lab infrastructure facilities at all our training centers across the city. Our lab facilities are available throughout the day on all working days. For our online students, they can connect to our servers and other lab facilities over the internet and practice. These facilities are available 24X7.

What if I miss one (or more) class?

If the student misses attending any session, he or she can re-attend the session by:
1. Attending the same session in another batch if the student is attending a classroom-based session.
2. For online sessions, recordings of the classes can be accessed by the student at all times to help revisit and listen to the missed sessions.

What are the modes of training offered for this course?

All training programs conducted by Ashok IT are available in 3 modes: instructor-based classroom programs, instructor-based live online training, and self-paced video-based training. Students can choose according to their preferences.
